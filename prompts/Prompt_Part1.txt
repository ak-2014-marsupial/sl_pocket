## БАЗА ЗНАНЬ (Part 1):

[Topic 01: Connecting with Others]
- Vocab (Full): How are you doing?=Як справи?; Can't complain=Все добре (не можу скаржитися); Same old=Все по-старому; Come from=Бути родом з; Work as=Працювати (кимось); Journalist=Журналіст; A bit=Трохи.
- Grammar: Present Simple (Facts): S+V(s/es). Present Continuous (Current): S+am/is/are+Ving. Past Simple (Origins): I was born in...
- Practice: How are you doing?; Where do you come from?; I was born in Spain; I come from Ukraine but now I’m living in Canada; What do you do?; I work as a journalist; Why are you learning English?; How many languages do you speak?; I’m doing well, thanks. How about you?; Not bad, just a bit busy with work; Чим ви займаєтесь?; Скількома мовами ви розмовляєте?; Я народився у Києві, але зараз живу у Львові.

[Topic 02: Different Families]
- Vocab (Full): Spouse=чоловік або дружина; Sibling=рідний брат або сестра; An only child=єдина дитина; Niece=племінниця; Nephew=племінник; Mother-in-law=свекруха/теща; Father-in-law=свекор/тесть; Child-free family=сім’я без дітей; Family member=член сім'ї; To give birth=народити дитину.
- Grammar: Family (is) - unit. Family members (are) - individuals.
- Practice: After getting married, she became my spouse; My sister gave birth to a baby girl; They decided to be a child-free family; My father-in-law is very kind; I am an only child; My family members are visiting; His sister’s son is a wonderful nephew; Maria is close to her mother-in-law; My sister and I are very close; Emily is an only child; Sam’s sibling is Lily; Emily’s cousin is Sam; I get along quite well with my mother-in-law.

[Topic 03: Past Continuous]
- Vocab (Full): All day / The whole day=Весь день; While=В той час як / поки; At 6 PM (yesterday)=О 6 годині вечора; At the same time=В цей же час; Sibling=Брат або сестра; Spouse=Чоловік або дружина; Pouring (rain)=Сильна злива.
- Grammar: S + was/were + V-ing. Spelling: make->making, die->dying, run->running.
- Practice: At 6 p.m. last night, we were not watching a movie; They were playing football while their mum was cooking; I was reading a book, and my brother was listening to music; Yesterday at noon, he was chatting with his friends; The children were running while their parents were sitting on a bench; The students were preparing for the exams at 10 a.m.; I wasn’t playing computer games at midnight; They were swimming in the pool at 3 p.m.; We weren’t traveling to Paris this time last year; I was doing my homework all day yesterday; What were you doing this time yesterday?; My friend was studying while I was watching TikTok; She was preparing for the exams all night; We were having dinner yesterday in the evening.

[Topic 04: Emotions & Feelings]
- Vocab (Full): Excited=В захваті; Nervous=Знервований; Upset=Засмучений; Afraid=Наляканий; Angry=Злий; Tired=Втомлений; Awesome=Чудовий / Класний; Amazing=Дивовижний; Awful=Жахливий.
- Grammar: Happy/Excited/Nervous/Upset + ABOUT; Angry + WITH (person) / ABOUT (thing); Afraid/Tired + OF.
- Practice: I was so happy about winning the contest; She was upset about losing her favorite book; The weather was awful during our trip; She was nervous about the test; I’m really nervous about the exam tomorrow; I was upset that I couldn’t go; I’m afraid I would get quite frustrated; To be honest, I might feel a bit relieved.

[Topic 05: Family Holidays]
- Vocab (Full): Family traditions=Сімейні традиції; To celebrate=Святкувати; To exchange gifts=Обмінюватися подарунками; To get together=Збиратися разом; Traditional dishes=Традиційні страви; To decorate=Прикрашати; Baubles=Ялинкові кульки; String lights=Гірлянда; Easter basket=Великодній кошик; Fireworks=Феєрверки; To bring joy=Приносити радість.
- Grammar: AT Christmas (period); ON Christmas Day (specific day).
- Practice: Every year, we follow our traditions; On Christmas Day, we exchange presents; During Easter, we put eggs in the basket; The string lights sparkle beautifully; baubles make the Christmas tree look amazing; Our grandmother makes traditional dishes like borscht; Holidays always bring joy; I really enjoy the cozy atmosphere; I’m afraid we don’t have many strict traditions.

[Topic 06: Past Simple vs Past Continuous]
- Vocab (Full): Completed action=Завершена дія; Series of actions=Послідовність дій; Interrupted action=Перервана дія; Simultaneous actions=Одночасні дії; Specific time=Конкретний час; To occur=Траплятися, відбуватися.
- Grammar: Past Simple (V2) for short actions. Past Continuous (was/were+Ving) for long actions. Combined: [Past Cont] + WHEN + [Past Simple].
- Practice: I was watching TV when you called me; While we were walking, it started raining; She finished her homework before she went to bed; They were playing football when it began to snow; He was reading a book when his phone rang; I wasn’t listening to the teacher when she asked a question; The sun was shining when we left the house; She was cooking dinner while he was doing his homework; We were having dinner when the power went out; She fell while I was chasing her; I was having tea when he came; Коли вони прийшли, ми вже вечеряли; Він чистив зуби, коли почув гучний звук; Ми планували подорож, коли отримали новини.

[Topic 07: World of Traits]
- Vocab (Full): Outgoing=Комунікабельний; Confident=Впевнений; Honest=Чесний; Hard-working=Працьовитий; Generous=Щедрий; Selfish=Егоїстичний; Mean=Скупий / Жорстокий; Rude=Грубий; Patient=Терплячий; Impatient=Нетерплячий.
- Grammar: S + be + (adverb) + Adjective. Use softeners for negative traits: "a bit rude".
- Practice: She loves meeting new people, she is very outgoing; He stays calm and waits his turn, he is patient; She always tells the truth, she is honest; He only thinks about himself, he is selfish; She works late every night, she is hard-working; That was very rude of her; He believes in himself, he is confident; To be honest, I really enjoy meeting new people; To be fair, it's not always easy to be honest.

## БАЗА ЗНАНЬ (Part 2):

[Topic 23: Get Phrases]
- Vocab (Full): To get (receive)=Отримувати; To get (buy)=Купувати; To get (reach)=Добиратися; To get (become)=Ставати (переходити в стан); Get better=Одужувати / Ставати краще; Get bored=Занудьгувати; Get angry with=Розсердитися на когось; Get hungry=Зголодніти; Get ill=Захворіти; Get worried=Розхвилюватися; Get thirsty=Хотіти пити; Get tired of=Втомитися від чогось.
- Grammar: Subject + Get + Adjective = process of change. "My English is getting better".
- Practice: I hope you get better soon; We got bored during the meeting; I got hungry; She got angry with her brother; He got tired of waiting; She started to get worried; I hope I don’t get ill; My English is getting better; I’m afraid I’m getting a bit worried; Actually, I got an email; I was starting to get a little angry with the lack of communication.

[Topic 24: Present Perfect (Ever/Never/Just)]
- Vocab (Full): Ever=Колись; Never=Ніколи; Just=Щойно; Already=Вже; Yet=Ще (не); To fail an exam=Провалити іспит; To get a promotion=Отримати підвищення; Unusual food=Незвичайна їжа; To fall asleep=Заснути.
- Grammar: S+have/has+V3. Expierence (Ever/Never). Recent actions (Just/Already/Yet).
- Practice: I haven't eaten sushi before; Have you met a famous person?; She has finished her homework; They haven't visited Paris; We have seen that movie twice; Have you tried the new restaurant?; I saw that movie last week; She hasn’t finished her homework yet; We met in 2010; They have bought a new car; I have just got a promotion; I have just missed the bus; To be honest, I have never tried sushi.

[Topic 25: Travel Experiences]
- Vocab (Full): Passport=Паспорт; Ticket=Квиток; Suitcase=Валіза; To book a trip/hotel=Бронювати поїздку/готель; To pack a suitcase=Збирати валізу; To go sightseeing=Оглядати визначні пам'ятки; To try local food=Куштувати місцеву їжу; To lie on a beach=Лежати на пляжі; To stay at a hotel=Зупинятися в готелі; Valid=Дійсний.
- Grammar: Giving Advice with SHOULD / SHOULDN'T. Present Perfect for travel experiences.
- Practice: To book a hotel for the weekend; relax and lie on a beach; Make sure you have your passport; travel by train; One of the best parts is trying local food; I always pack my suitcase early; go sightseeing at famous places; buy a ticket before getting on the train; stay at a hotel at a cozy resort; go on a trip to the mountains; Is your passport valid?; You have lost your ticket; I don't know how to pack a suitcase.

[Topic 26: Present Perfect (How long)]
- Vocab (Full): Anniversary=Річниця; Since=З (певного часу); For=Протягом / впродовж; Experience=Досвід; Unemployed=Безробітний; Kindergarten=Дитячий садок.
- Grammar: Since (point in time) vs For (period). Been to (visited) vs Been in (still there).
- Practice: I have known Bob for 5 years; She has lived in Kyiv since 2010; They have worked here for 2 months; We have been friends since childhood; He has studied English for 3 years; I have had a dog since 2017; My parents have been married for 20 years; They have known each other since high school; She has been my best friend for 10 years; Jack has lived in London since he was born; Bill has been unemployed since April; Anna has been sick since Monday; I’ve been in this house since last summer.

[Topic 27: Staying in a Hotel]
- Vocab (Full): Check in=Зареєструватися; Check out=Виписатися; Hotel lobby=Вестибюль/хол; Reception=Рецепція; AC (Air Conditioning)=Кондиціонер; Room service=Обслуговування номерів; Elevator / Lift=Ліфт; Breakfast included=Сніданок включено; Single bed=Односпальне ліжко; Double bed=Двоспальне ліжко; Twin beds=Два окремі ліжка; Reservation=Бронювання.
- Grammar: Pay IN cash / Pay BY card. Reservation UNDER the name. I'd like to (polite).
- Practice: we went to the reception to check in; lobby has comfortable chairs; We need to check out by 11 AM; if breakfast is included; call room-service; elevator/lift doesn't work; turned on the AC; reservation under the name Johnson; ID and a credit card; pay by card or cash; I'm afraid the AC isn't working; Could you tell me what the check-in time is?

[Topic 28: Weather]
- Vocab (Full): Weather forecast=Прогноз погоди; Foggy=Туманно; Windy=Вітряно; Cloudy=Хмарно; It’s snowing=Сніжить; It’s raining=Дощить; Boiling hot=Дуже спекотно; Freezing cold=Дуже холодно (морозно); Degrees Celsius=Градуси Цельсія; Thunderstorm=Гроза; Lightning=Блискавка.
- Grammar: It is + adjective (State). It is + verb-ing (Now). Predictions: It will + verb.
- Practice: It’s very sunny, so it is hot; There are dark clouds, it is cloudy; It’s minus 18, it is cold; There is a thunderstorm; You need to drive with lights on because it is foggy; It’s very hot and sunny today; The weather forecast says it will rain tomorrow; It’s cloudy outside, but it’s not cold; I usually check the weather forecast; I’d much rather have sunny and hot weather; I’m afraid I don’t handle low temperatures well.

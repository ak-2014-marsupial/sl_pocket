[Topic 11: Second Conditional & Hypotheses]
- Vocab (Full): Hypothetical=Гіпотетичний / Уявний; To melt=Танути; Invisible=Невидимий; To switch lives=Помінятися життями; To treat=Лікувати; Lottery=Лотерея.
- Grammar: Second Conditional (Imaginary): If + Past Simple, would + Verb. Use "were" for all subjects (If I were you).

[Topic 12: Our Amazing Bodies & Body Language]

[Topic 13: Shop Smart]

[Topic 14: Present Simple Passive]
- Vocab (Full): Passive Voice=Пасивний стан; Active Voice=Активний стан; To be driven=Бути підвезеним; Regularly=Регулярно; To be woken up=Бути розбудженим; To be asked=Бути запитаним.
- Grammar: Subject + am/is/are + V3. Use when the doer is unknown or unimportant.
- Practice: She is driven to school by her mother every day; She is woken up by her mother at 7 am; The waiter is asked by Sue to bring some water; The boxes are moved out of the way by Michael; The dog is walked by the boys every day; Actually, I’m quite fond of sunny weather; To be honest, I believe everything looks much better; I’m afraid I’m not a big fan of hot weather; It might sound unusual, but I actually prefer rainy days; tasks are usually distributed very fairly; Could I say that everyone is assigned roles according to their strengths?; a fresh bottle is being brought to your table; I was wondering if the bill is usually included?; I'm afraid it isn't, but it can be added.

[Topic 15: At the Store]
- Vocab (Full): Shop assistant=Продавець-консультант; Customer=Покупець; Fitting room=Примірочна; Receipt=Чек; Bill=Рахунок; To fit=Підходити (розмір); To suit=Пасувати (стиль).

- Practice: This suit fits you; This sweater suits you; I will take it; How can I help you?; I am looking for a red T-shirt; How much is this?; Can I try it on?; Have you got it in a different color?; To be honest, I'm finding it a bit hard to locate; Would you mind helping me find a pair in my size?; Actually, I really like this jacket; Might I try it on to see how it fits?; I'm afraid I can't see the price tag; Actually, I'm looking for a summer dress; Could I possibly try it on?.

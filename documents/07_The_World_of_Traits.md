# The World of Traits #

### 1. Vocabulary & Definitions

| Word/Phrase | Definition in English | Переклад |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Outgoing** | Friendly and energetic; enjoying the company of others. | Комунікабельний |
| **Confident** | Feeling sure about one's own abilities or qualities.  | Впевнений |
| **Honest** | Always telling the truth and never stealing or cheating. | Чесний |
| **Hard-working** | Putting a lot of effort and care into work.  | Працьовитий |
| **Generous** | Willing to give more money, help, or kindness than is usual. | Щедрий |
| **Selfish** | Caring only about oneself and not about others.  | Егоїстичний |
| **Mean** | Unkind or unpleasant; also unwilling to spend money. | Скупий / Жорстокий |
| **Rude** | Not polite; offensive or embarrassing in behavior.  | Грубий |
| **Patient** | Able to stay calm and wait without getting angry. | Терплячий |
| **Impatient** | Easily annoyed by having to wait for something. | Нетерплячий |

### 2. Grammar Essentials: Describing Personality


* **Formula:** `Subject + be + (adverb) + Adjective`
* **Examples from slides:**
* "She **is very** outgoing." 

* "He **stays** calm and waits his turn. He **is very** patient." 

* "She **can be a little** rude when someone interrupts her." 

---

### 3. Written Practice

1. She loves meeting new people and is never shy in social situations. She is very **outgoing**.
2. Even when things are tough, he stays calm and waits his turn. He is very **patient**.
3. She always tells the truth, no matter what. Everyone knows she is **honest**.
4. He never shares his toys with anyone and only thinks about himself. He is **selfish**.
5. She works late every night to make sure her projects are perfect. She is very **hard-working**.
6. He always gives money to charity and helps those in need. He is **generous**.
7. She made fun of her friend in front of everyone. That was very **rude** of her.
8. He believes in himself and never doubts his abilities. He is **confident**.
9. He never shares and is always unkind to others. He is **mean**.

### 4. Speaking & Discussion Guide

Q1: How do you feel when you meet new people? Do you enjoy it? 

* **Positive:** "To be honest, I really enjoy meeting new people. I find it quite exciting to hear different perspectives, and I'd say I'm a rather outgoing person."
* **Negative:** "Actually, I'm afraid I find it a bit stressful. I tend to be quite shy at first, so it might take me some time to feel comfortable."
* **Neutral:** "It really depends on the situation. If it's a professional setting, I'm usually quite confident, but in large social groups, I could be a little more reserved."

Q2: Is it always easy for you to be honest? Why or why not? 

* **Positive:** "Generally, I believe that being honest is the best policy. Even if the truth is difficult, I try to express it as politely as possible."
* **Negative:** "To be fair, it's not always easy. I'm sometimes worried about hurting someone's feelings, so I might use a 'white lie' to avoid being rude."

**Q3: In what situations do you feel most confident?** 

* "I feel most confident when I am well-prepared for a task. For instance, after studying a topic thoroughly, I would say I'm quite sure of my abilities."

### 5. Professional Scenarios (Dialogue Practice)

**Scenario: Discussing a Colleague (Based on "Alex" profile)**

* **A:** "Could you tell me what you think about Alex’s performance lately?"
* **B:** "Well, to be honest, he is incredibly **hard-working**. He often puts in extra hours to ensure everything is perfect. However, he can be a bit **impatient** when things don’t go his way. Perhaps we could suggest some ways to stay calm during busy periods?"

**Scenario: Describing a Friend (Based on "Iryna" profile)**

* **A:** "I'm looking for someone to help host the event. Is Iryna a good fit?"
* **B:** "Actually, she’d be perfect! She’s very **outgoing** and makes friends easily. Just keep in mind that she might be a little sensitive to interruptions, but overall, she is very **generous** with her time."

### 6. Polite English Cheat Sheet

* **Softeners (Пом'якшувачі):**
* *Actually / To be honest:* Використовуйте перед висловленням думки, яка може не збігатися з думкою співрозмовника.
* *A bit / A little:* Використовуйте перед негативними рисами (наприклад, "a bit rude"), щоб вони звучали менш образливо.


* **Modal Verbs (Модальні дієслова):**
* *Could / Would / Might:* Додають ввічливості проханням або припущенням.


* **Indirect Questions (Непрямі запитання):**
* *I was wondering if... / Could you tell me...:* Замість прямих запитань, щоб не звучати занадто різко.

## 1. Vocabulary & Definitions

| Word/Phrase | Definition in English | Переклад |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Gadget** | Small, useful electronic tools like phones or tablets. | Гаджет / пристрій |
| **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** | A smart computer program that can learn and make decisions. | Штучний інтелект (ШІ) |
| **Laptop** | A portable computer you can carry and use on your lap. | Ноутбук |
| **Tablet** | A thin, flat computer with a touch screen. | Планшет |
| **Screen** | The part of a device where you see images and information. | Екран |
| **Headphones** | A device you wear over your ears to listen to sounds privately. | Навушники |
| **Charger** | A tool used to add power to a device's battery. | Зарядний пристрій |
| **To browse (the Internet)** | To look for information on the web. | Переглядати (інтернет) |
| **To download** | To copy data or apps from the Internet to your device. | Завантажувати |
| **To log in** | To enter a system using a username and password. | Увійти в систему |

## 3. Grammar Essentials

**Formula: Verb + Object (Technical Collocations)**

Цей розділ фокусується на стійких словосполученнях для опису технічних дій у професійному середовищі.

* *Example: **Type** a password / an email address.*
* *Example: **Click** on an icon / a link.*
* *Example: **Send** a message / a link.*
* *Example: **Charge** a phone / a battery.*

**Polite Grammar Tip:**
Використовуйте модальні дієслова **could** або **would** для прохань, щоб звучати ввічливіше.

* *Example: **Would you mind** if I borrowed your charger for a moment?*

## 4. Written Practice

### Exercise 1: Choose the correct option

1. I use my **tablet** to watch movies when I travel.
2. If my phone battery is low, I need to use a **charger**.
3. To access my email, I need to **type** my password.
4. My friend sent me a photo; I’ll **click** on it to see it in full size.
5. I often **browse** the Internet to find interesting recipes.
6. You need to **download** an app before you can use it on your phone.
7. When I have a question, I **call** my friend for help.
8. Make sure to visit our **website** to learn more about the product.
9. Please enter your **email address** so we can contact you.
10. If you want to listen to music without disturbing others, use your **headphones**.

### Translation Block

* **UA:** Я заряджаю свій телефон, бо забув свій зарядний пристрій вдома.
**EN: I’m charging my phone because I forgot my charger at home.**
* **UA:** Моя сестра любить слухати музику через навушники, коли працює.
**EN: My sister likes to listen to music with headphones while she works.**
* **UA:** Я завжди перевіряю свою електронну пошту, коли заходжу на сайт університету.
**EN: I always check my email when I go to the university website.**
* **UA:** Мій новий планшет має великий екран, і це зручно для перегляду фільмів.
**EN: My new tablet has a large screen, and it’s convenient for watching movies.**
* **UA:** Він не знав свій пароль, тому не зміг увійти до системи.
**EN: He didn’t know his password, so he couldn’t log in.**

## 5. Speaking & Discussion Guide

**Q1: What gadget do you use the most, and why?**

* **Positive:** *To be honest*, I’d have to say my smartphone. It’s incredibly versatile, and I use it for everything from work emails to navigation.
* **Neutral:** *Actually*, I use my laptop and phone almost equally. While the phone is great for quick tasks, I find the laptop much more efficient for focused work.
* **Negative:** *I'm afraid* I rely on my laptop far too much. It's quite powerful, but it's not very portable, which can be frustrating when I'm on the move.

**Q2: How often do you need to charge your phone? Do you always carry a charger?**

* **Negative:** *I’m afraid* my battery life isn't very good, so I usually have to charge it twice a day. Because of that, I always make sure to carry a charger in my bag.
* **Positive:** *Actually*, my phone has a fairly strong battery, so I only charge it once at night. I don't usually carry a charger unless I'm traveling.
* **Neutral:** *To be honest*, I charge it whenever I see the battery drop below 30%. I might carry a power bank instead of a charger just in case.

**Q3: What are your favorite apps to download?**

* **Positive:** *I might suggest* trying educational apps like Duolingo. *To be honest*, they really help me stay productive during my daily commute.
* **Negative:** *I'm afraid* I don't download many new apps lately. *Actually*, I find that most of them take up too much space without offering much value.
* **Neutral:** *To be honest*, I mostly download utility apps. *I might suggest* looking for cloud storage apps if you need to access files on multiple devices.

## 6. Professional Scenarios

**Context: Technical Support in the Office**

**Nina:** Excuse me, Mark. *I’m afraid* I forgot my charger at home. **Could** I possibly borrow yours to charge my phone for an hour?

**Mark:** *Of course!* It’s right here in my bag. By the way, **would you mind** telling me if those are new headphones? I saw you using them earlier.

**Nina:** *Actually*, yes! They are noise-canceling, which is quite helpful in a noisy office.

## 7. Polite English Cheat Sheet

| Phrase / Softener | When to use it (Context) |
| --- | --- |
| **Actually...** | Для ввічливого виправлення або уточнення інформації. |
| **I’m afraid...** | Щоб пом'якшити погані новини або відмову. |
| **To be honest...** | Коли ви висловлюєте свою особисту думку або зізнаєтеся в чомусь. |
| **Could you / Would you...** | Використовуйте замість наказів для створення ввічливих запитів. |
| **I might suggest...** | Для надання поради, яка не звучить занадто нав'язливо. |

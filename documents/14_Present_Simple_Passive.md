# 14. Present Simple Passive

## 1. Vocabulary & Definitions

| Word/Phrase | Definition in English | Переклад |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Launch** | To start or introduce a new product, service, or project. | Запускати (продукт) |
| **Submit** | To give a document or a report to someone in authority. | Подавати (звіт/документ) |
| **Sweep** | To clean a floor using a brush or broom. | Підмітати |
| **Check** | To examine something to see if it is correct or in good condition. | Перевіряти |
| **Bake** | To cook food, like bread or cakes, in an oven. | Пекти/Випікати |
| **Deliver** | To take letters or goods to people's houses or places of work. | Доставляти |
| **Direct** | To control the movement or flow of something, like traffic. | Направляти / Регулювати |

## 2. Vocabulary Hooks

> **Submit:** Think of "submitting" as "sending under" the boss's door for review at the end of the day.

## 3. Grammar Essentials

**Present Simple Passive** is used when the action itself is more important than who performs it, or when the performer is unknown.

**Formula:** `Subject + am/is/are + Verb3/Verb-ed` 

**Positive:** *The house is cleaned once a week by Paul*.

**Negative:** *The meal isn't cooked by the chef*.

**Question:** *Is the meal cooked by the chef?* 


**Usage (UA):** Ми використовуємо пасивний стан, коли фокусуємося на об'єкті, над яким виконується дія. Якщо ми хочемо зазначити, хто саме виконує дію, ми додаємо прийменник **by**. Важливо пам'ятати, що не всі речення можна перетворити на пасив: якщо дієслово неперехідне (немає додатка, на який спрямована дія), пасивний стан неможливий.

* Paul drives to work every day. $\rightarrow$ **It cannot be changed (Intransitive verb)**.

* Sue asks a waiter to bring some water. $\rightarrow$ **The waiter is asked by Sue to bring some water**.

* Sarah walks on the beach regularly. $\rightarrow$ **It cannot be changed**.

## 5. Speaking & Discussion Guide

Q: How is the work organized in a typical bakery every morning? 

**Positive:** *Actually, it is a very well-structured process.* Fresh bread is prepared by the baker, and all ingredients are mixed early so the bread is baked in time for customers.

**Neutral:** *To be honest, it’s quite standard.* The bakery is cleaned by the staff, and then the bread is picked up by the delivery person.

**Negative:** *I’m afraid it can be quite stressful.* If the bread is not bought quickly by customers, the baker’s hard work is sometimes wasted.


Q: How are tasks handled in your office or school? 

**Positive:** *I believe everything is handled efficiently.* Homework is given every week, and all reports are checked by the manager regularly.

**Neutral:** *As far as I know, it's quite routine.* Emails are sent every morning, and the rooms are cleaned by the staff daily.

**Negative:** *Actually, I’m concerned about the timing.* Sometimes documents are not signed on time, even though the project is finished according to the schedule.



## 6. Professional Scenarios

**Context: Business Operations**
**Scenario:**

**Role A (Project Lead):** Could you please confirm if the new product is launched yet? 


* **Role B (Marketing):** *Actually, I'm afraid* it is still being finalized. However, the invitations are already sent by the event coordinator.

* **Role A:** I see. Would you mind checking if the reports are submitted to the manager? 

**Role B:** *To be honest,* they are checked right now and will be delivered by the end of the hour.



## 7. Polite English Cheat Sheet

| Phrase / Softener | When to use it (Context) |
| --- | --- |
| **Actually...** | To politely correct information or add a specific detail. |
| **I’m afraid...** | To soften the delivery of negative news or obstacles. |
| **To be honest...** | To introduce a sincere or direct opinion. |
| **Could you... / Would you mind...** | To turn a direct command into a polite request. |
| **As far as I know...** | To share information while acknowledging you might not have the full picture.|

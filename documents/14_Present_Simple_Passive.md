# Present Simple Passive

## 1. Vocabulary & Definitions

| Word/Phrase | Definition in English | Переклад |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Passive Voice** | A grammatical construction where the subject is the recipient of the action. | Пасивний стан |
| **Active Voice** | A construction where the subject performs the action. | Активний стан |
| **To be driven** | To be transported by a vehicle managed by another person. | Бути підвезеним (кимось) |
| **Regularly** | Doing something at fixed intervals or frequently. | Регулярно |
| **To be woken up** | To be caused to stop sleeping by someone or something. | Бути розбудженим |
| **To be asked** | To be requested to do something or provide information. | Бути запитаним / Отримати прохання |

---

## 2. Grammar Essentials: Present Simple Passive

Пасивний стан використовується, коли фокус уваги зосереджений на об'єкті дії, а не на тому, хто її виконує.

**Formula:**

> **Subject + am / is / are + Verb (3rd form / -ed)**

**Usage:**

* Коли виконавець невідомий або неважливий.
* Коли ми хочемо звучати більш офіційно або об'єктивно.

**Examples from the slides:**

* *Active:* They make coffee. $\rightarrow$ *Passive:* **Coffee is made.**
* *Active:* Her mother wakes her up. $\rightarrow$ *Passive:* **She is woken up by her mother.**

## 3. Written Practice (Completed Exercises)

1. Her mother drives her to school every day. $\rightarrow$ **She is driven to school by her mother every day.**
2. Paul drives to work every day. $\rightarrow$ *It cannot be changed (Intransitive verb).*
3. I wake up late on Sunday mornings. $\rightarrow$ *It cannot be changed.*
4. Her mother wakes her up at 7 am. $\rightarrow$ **She is woken up by her mother at 7 am.**
5. Sue asks a waiter to bring some water. $\rightarrow$ **The waiter is asked by Sue to bring some water.**
6. David always asks for some help. $\rightarrow$ *It cannot be changed.*
7. Simon moves to Italy. $\rightarrow$ *It cannot be changed.*
8. Michael moves boxes out of the way. $\rightarrow$ **The boxes are moved out of the way by Michael.**
9. Sarah walks on the beach regularly. $\rightarrow$ *It cannot be changed.*
10. The boys walk the dog every day. $\rightarrow$ **The dog is walked by the boys every day.**

## 4. Speaking & Discussion Guide

### **Topic: Daily Routines & Preferences**

**Question: What kind of weather do you prefer?**

* **Positive:** *Actually, I’m quite fond of sunny weather because it makes me feel more energetic. To be honest, I believe everything looks much better when the sun is shining.*
* **Negative:** *I’m afraid I’m not a big fan of hot weather. It might sound unusual, but I actually prefer rainy days as they are perfect for staying productive at home.*
* **Neutral:** *To be fair, I don’t mind any kind of weather as long as I’m dressed appropriately. However, a mild spring day would probably be my first choice.*

**Question: How are tasks usually distributed in your household/office?**

* **Positive:** *In our office, tasks are usually distributed very fairly. Could I say that everyone is assigned roles according to their strengths, which makes the process efficient?*
* **Negative:** *To be honest, it seems that some people are given more work than others. I’m afraid the balance isn’t quite right at the moment.*
* **Neutral:** *Actually, tasks are simply listed in our shared system, and they are picked up by team members as they become available.*

## 5. Professional Scenarios (Adapted Dialogues)

**Scenario: In a Restaurant (Based on "Asking a waiter")**

* **Guest:** "Excuse me, **could I possibly** have some more water?"
* **Waiter:** "Of course. **Actually**, a fresh bottle is being brought to your table right now."
* **Guest:** "Thank you. **I was wondering if** the bill is usually included in the service charge?"
* **Waiter:** "**I'm afraid** it isn't, but it can be added if you wish."

## 6. Polite English Cheat Sheet

| Phrase / Softener | How and when to use it |
| --- | --- |
| **"Actually..."** | Використовуйте для ввічливого виправлення або додавання нової інформації, щоб не звучати занадто прямолінійно. |
| **"I’m afraid..."** | Додається перед повідомленням поганих новин або відмовою, щоб пом'якшити удар. |
| **"To be honest / To be fair..."** | Використовується для висловлення особистої думки, особливо якщо вона може бути непопулярною. |
| **"Could / Would / Might"** | Модальні дієслова, що перетворюють наказ на ввічливе прохання або припущення. |
| **"I was wondering if..."** | Найкращий спосіб почати непряме запитання, щоб не тиснути на співрозмовника. |

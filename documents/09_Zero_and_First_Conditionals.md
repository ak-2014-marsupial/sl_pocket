## 2. Grammar Essentials

### **Zero Conditional (Нульовий тип)**


**Formula:** `If/When + Present Simple, Present Simple`.

**Usage:** Використовується для фактів, наукових законів, правил та речей, які завжди є правдивими (100% вірогідність).


* **Examples:**
* If you freeze water, it turns into ice.

* If you don't water plants, they die.





### **First Conditional (Перший тип)**

 
**Formula:** `If/When + Present Simple, will/won't + Verb`.

**Usage:** Описує реальні або можливі ситуації в теперішньому чи майбутньому (50-90% вірогідність).


* **Examples:**
* If it rains, I will stay at home.


* If she studies hard, she will pass the exam.

## 3. Written Practice

Exercise 1: Zero Conditional Completion 

1. If you **don't drink** enough water, you **get** dehydrated.
2. My teacher **gets** angry if I **don't study**.
3. If I **don't know** a word in English, I **look** it up in my dictionary.
4. If my mother **drinks** coffee before she goes to bed, she **can't sleep**.
5. If you **recycle**, you **help** the environment.
6. If you **take** medicine when you are sick, you **feel** better.

Exercise 2: Sentence Matching (First Conditional) 

* If you don’t drink water, you will be thirsty.
* If you work hard, you will succeed.
* If it snows tonight, we will build a snowman.
* If she doesn’t study, she will fail the test.
* If I have time, I will help you with your project.

Exercise 3: Correct the Mistakes 

1. If you **heat** water to 100°C, it boils. (*Виправлено: видалено "will" у частині з if*)
2. If she studies hard, she **will pass** the exam. (*Виправлено: додано "will" для майбутнього результату*)
3. If I eat fruits and veggies, I **will not get** sick. (*Виправлено: додано "will"*)
4. If you **do not** hurry, you **will miss** the bus. (*Виправлено: "will" не вживається після "if"*)
5. If it rains tomorrow, we **will go** to the cinema. (*Виправлено: додано "will"*)

## 4. Speaking & Discussion Guide

1. If you feel stressed, what do you usually do? 

* *Positive:* To be honest, I usually try to meditate or listen to some calm music. It actually helps me clear my mind.
* *Neutral:* If I feel stressed, I might take a short walk. I’m afraid it doesn’t always work, but it’s better than doing nothing.

2. When you’re tired, what helps you relax? 

* *Response:* I would say that reading a book is the best way for me to relax. Actually, I find it much more helpful than watching TV.

3. If you win the lottery, what will you do with the money? 

* *Positive:* If I were lucky enough to win, I would probably donate a portion to charity and then travel the world.
* *Negative:* To be fair, I don’t think I’ll ever win, but if I did, I might just save most of it for the future.

4. If you have a free day tomorrow, how will you spend it? 

* *Response:* I’m not entirely sure, but I could potentially visit my relatives or go to the countryside if the weather stays nice.

## 5. Scenario-Based Dialogue

**Situation:** Two colleagues are discussing a project deadline.

**A:** Could you tell me if you'll finish the report by tomorrow?

**B:** To be honest, I’m afraid I might need more time. If I finish the data analysis tonight, I’ll definitely send it to you first thing in the morning.

**A:** I understand. Actually, if you need any help, I would be happy to assist you with the final slides.

**B:** That would be wonderful, thank you! If we work together, we'll surely meet the deadline.

## 6. Polite English Cheat Sheet

| Category | Phrases Used | When to use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Softeners** | *Actually, To be honest, To be fair, I’m afraid* | Щоб пом'якшити відмову або зробити твердження менш різким. |
| **Modal Verbs** | *Could, Would, Might* | Щоб ваші пропозиції чи припущення звучали менш нав'язливо. |
| **Probability** | *Probably, Potentially, I would say* | Щоб уникнути занадто прямолінійних відповідей ("I don't know"). |
| **Indirectness** | *I'm not entirely sure... but...* | Коли ви хочете висловити невпевненість ввічливо. |

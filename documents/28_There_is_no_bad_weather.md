
# THERE IS NO BAD WEATHER

## I. Vocabulary & Definitions


| Word / Phrase | Definition in English | Переклад українською |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Weather forecast** | A statement saying what the weather will be like soon. | Прогноз погоди |
| **Foggy** | Difficult to see because of low clouds near the ground. | Туманно |
| **Windy** | With a lot of strong moving air. | Вітряно |
| **Cloudy** | When the sky is covered with clouds. | Хмарно |
| **It’s snowing** | Frozen water falling from the sky. | Сніжить |
| **It’s raining** | Water falling from the sky in drops. | Дощить |
| **Boiling hot** | Extremely hot weather (informal). | Дуже спекотно |
| **Freezing cold** | Extremely cold weather. | Дуже холодно (морозно) |
| **Degrees Celsius** | Units for measuring temperature. | Градуси Цельсія |
| **Thunderstorm** | A storm with thunder and lightning. | Гроза |
| **Lightning** | A bright flash of light in the sky. | Блискавка |

## II. Grammar Essentials

1. **Current State (Adjectives):** `It is (It's) + adjective`.
* *Example:* It’s foggy today.


2. **Ongoing Process (Verbs):** `It is + verb-ing`.
* *Example:* It’s raining outside.


3. **Future Predictions:** `The weather forecast says it will + verb`.
* *Example:* The forecast says it will snow tomorrow.

## III. Written Practice (Completed Exercises)


1. It’s very sunny, so it is **hot**.
2. There are dark clouds, and it will rain soon; it is **cloudy**.
3. It’s minus 18 degrees Celsius, and you need a warm coat because it is **cold**.
4. The sky is clear, and there’s no wind, so the weather is **warm**.
5. There are heavy rains with lightning, so there is a **thunderstorm**.
6. You can’t see much and you need to drive with the lights on because it is **foggy**.
7. **It’s very hot and sunny today.** (Сьогодні дуже жарко і сонячно).
8. **The weather forecast says it will rain tomorrow.** (Прогноз погоди каже, що завтра буде дощити).
9. **It’s cloudy outside, but it’s not cold.** (На вулиці хмарно, але ще не холодно).

## IV. Open Discussion & Speaking Scenarios


**1. Do you usually check the weather forecast?**

> "Yes, **I usually tend to** check the weather forecast every morning. It helps me decide what to wear. **To be honest**, I find it quite helpful for planning my day."

**2. What kind of weather do you prefer: sunny and hot or cold and cloudy?**

> "Actually, **I’d much rather** have sunny and hot weather because it makes me feel energetic. However, **I must admit** that if it’s *boiling hot*, I prefer staying indoors."

**3. How do you feel about the weather when it’s cold? Do you enjoy it, or would you rather stay inside?**

> "I’d say it depends. If it’s just chilly, I enjoy a walk. But when it’s *freezing cold*, **I would definitely prefer** to stay inside. **I’m afraid** I don’t handle low temperatures very well."

**4. How do you usually check the weather forecast before you go out?**

> "**In most cases**, I prefer using a mobile app. It’s very convenient. **I was wondering**, do you find weather apps to be accurate?"

**5. Do you like going for a walk when it’s foggy? Why or why not?**

> "**To be honest**, I’d rather stay at home when it’s foggy. It feels a bit gloomy. **I’d much prefer** a clear, sunny day for a walk."

**6. How do you usually dress when the temperature is around 20°C?**

> "If it's around 20 degrees, **I usually tend to** wear light layers, like a shirt and a light jacket. **I find that** it's perfect for this temperature."

## V. Polite English Cheat Sheet

*Фрази-"пом'якшувачі" для професійного спілкування.*

* **I was wondering...** (Мені було цікаво... / Хотів запитати...) — для ввічливих запитань.
* **I’m afraid...** (Боюсь, що...) — для ввічливого повідомлення неприємних новин (наприклад, про дощ).
* **Would it be possible to...?** (Чи було б можливо...?) — для прохань.
* **Actually / To be honest...** (Власне / Якщо чесно...) — для висловлення власної думки.
* **I would say that...** (Я б сказав, що...) — щоб думка не звучала занадто категорично.

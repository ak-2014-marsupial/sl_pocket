# Past Simple vs Past Continuous #

### 1. Vocabulary & Definitions

| Word/Phrase | Definition in English | Переклад українською |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Completed action** | An action that is finished and over in the past. | Завершена дія |
| **Series of actions** | A list of events that happened one after another. | Послідовність дій |
| **Interrupted action** | A continuous action that was stopped by a shorter event. | Перервана дія |
| **Simultaneous actions** | Two or more things happening at the same time. | Одночасні дії |
| **Specific time** | A precise moment (e.g., at 5 pm). | Конкретний час |
| **To occur** | To happen or take place. | Траплятися, відбуватися |

---

### 2. Grammar Essentials


#### **A. Past Simple**

Використовується для завершених дій, фактів або звичок у минулому.

**Formula:** `Subject + V2 / V-ed`.
 
**Example:** *To be honest, I lived in Germany two years ago.*.

#### **B. Past Continuous**

Використовується для тривалих дій, що відбувалися в конкретний момент або паралельно з іншими діями.

**Formula:** `Subject + was/were + V-ing`.

**Example:** *Actually, I was cooking dinner at 5 pm yesterday.*.


#### **C. Combined Usage (Interruption)**

Коли коротка дія перериває тривалу.

* **Formula:** `Past Continuous + when + Past Simple`.

**Example:** *I was sleeping when the phone rang.*.

### 3. Written Practice

* **Mixed Tenses Exercise:**
1. I **was watching** TV when you **called** me.

2. While we **were walking** to school, it **started** raining.

3. She **finished** her homework before she **went** to bed.

4. They **were playing** football when it **began** to snow.

5. He **was reading** a book when his phone **rang**.

6. They **were driving** to the store when they **saw** an accident.

7. I **wasn’t listening** to the teacher when she **asked** me a question.

8. The sun **was shining** when we **left** the house.

9. She **was cooking** dinner while he **was doing** his homework.

10. We **were having** dinner when the power **went** out.


* **Sentence Construction:**
1. She **fell** while I **was chasing** her.

2. They **were cooking** the dinner and we **were setting** the table.

3. He **was singing** and we **were dancing**.

4. They **were talking** about the trip while we **were laughing**.

5. I **woke** up, **brushed** my teeth and then **ate** my breakfast.



* **Translation Block:**
* Я пив чай, коли він приїхав до мене — *I **was having** tea when he **came**.*.

* Коли вони приїхали, ми вже вечеряли — *We **were having** dinner when he **arrived**.*.

* Він чистив зуби, коли раптом почула гучний звук — *He **was brushing** his teeth when she **heard** a loud sound.*.

* Ми планували подорож, коли отримали новини — *We **were planning** the trip when they **got** the news.*.

* Вони обговорювали фільм, коли я увійшов у кімнату — *They **were discussing** a movie when I **entered** the room.*.

### 4. Speaking & Discussion Guide

**Topic: What were you doing when...?**

1. **Question: What were you doing when the power went out?**
* *Positive:* "To be honest, I was just finishing my work, so I was quite happy to take a break and light some candles."
* *Negative:* "I’m afraid I was in the middle of a very important Zoom meeting, so it was actually quite a stressful moment for me."
* *Neutral:* "Actually, I was just reading a book by the window, so I didn't mind it that much, to be fair."


2. **Scenario: You missed a teacher's question because you weren't listening.**
* *Polite Dialogue:*
* **Teacher:** "So, what is the answer to the second question?"
* **Student:** "I’m terribly sorry, could you please repeat that? I was actually trying to find the right page in my notebook and I might have missed your point."

3. **Scenario: Discussing a trip.**
* *Polite Dialogue:*
* **A:** "We were planning our trip to Italy when we heard the news about the flight changes."
* **B:** "Oh, that must have been frustrating. Would you mind telling me how you managed to resolve that situation?"

### 5. Polite English Cheat Sheet

* **To be honest / To be fair:** Використовуйте, щоб пом'якшити висловлення власної думки (наприклад, коли ви з чимось не згодні або визнаєте помилку).
* **Actually:** Чудовий спосіб ввічливо виправити когось або додати уточнення без прямого протистояння.
* **I'm afraid...:** Використовуйте перед тим, як повідомити погані новини або відмовити у проханні.
* **Could you / Would you mind...:** Завжди використовуйте ці модальні дієслова замість наказового способу для прохань.
* **I might have...:** Використовуйте, щоб зробити своє твердження менш категоричним (наприклад, "I might have misunderstood you").

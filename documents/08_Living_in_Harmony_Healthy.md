# Living in Harmony: Healthy Lifestyle #

---

### **1. Vocabulary & Definitions**


| Word / Phrase | Definition in English | Переклад |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **A balanced diet** | A diet that contains the right amounts of different foods to stay healthy. | Збалансоване харчування |
| **Mental health** | A person’s condition with regard to their psychological and emotional well-being. | Психічне здоров'я |
| **Physical health** | The state of your body and how well all your organs and systems are working. | Фізичне здоров'я |
| **To stay active** | To maintain a lifestyle that includes regular movement and physical activity. | Бути/залишатись активним |
| **Junk food** | Unhealthy food that is quick and easy to prepare, often high in fat or sugar. | Шкідлива їжа |
| **To skip meals** | To not eat one of the main meals of the day (breakfast, lunch, or dinner). | Пропускати прийоми їжі |
| **To get enough sleep** | To sleep for a sufficient number of hours to feel rested (usually 7-9 hours). | Висипатись |
| **To stay up late** | To go to bed much later than the usual or appropriate time. | Засиджуватись допізна |
| **To drink plenty of water** | To consume a large and sufficient amount of water daily. | Пити багато води |
| **To exercise regularly** | To do physical activity consistently as a part of a routine. | Регулярно робити вправи |

---

### **2. Grammar Essentials: Healthy Habits & Collocations**

* **Verbs for Positive Habits:**
* *To have/maintain* + a balanced diet.
* *To get* + enough sleep.
* *To drink* + plenty of water.
* *To take care of* + mental health.


* **Verbs for Negative Habits:**
* *To eat* + junk food.
* *To skip* + meals.
* *To stay up* + late.



**Polite Examples (Intermediate):**

* *I'm afraid* I don't always get enough sleep because of my work schedule.
* *Actually*, I’ve been trying to have a more balanced diet lately.
* *Could you please* tell me if you find it difficult to stay active during the day?

### **3. Written Practice**

1. Eating a **balanced diet** helps you get all the nutrients you need.
2. It’s important to **exercise regularly** every day to keep your body strong.
3. **Junk food** like chips and candy can lead to health problems.
4. Drinking **plenty of water** is necessary, especially during hot weather.
5. Staying active and eating well is part of a **healthy lifestyle**.
6. To improve your **mental health**, try to relax and do things you enjoy.
7. You should not **stay up late** if you want to feel fresh and alert in the morning.
8. **Skipping meals** can make you feel weak and dizzy during the day.
9. Going for a walk every day is a good way to **stay active**.
10. To feel good and have energy, it’s important to **get enough sleep** every night.

### **4. Speaking & Discussion Guide**

**Question 1: What do you usually eat for breakfast? Do you think it’s a healthy choice?**

* **Positive:** "To be honest, I usually have oatmeal with some fresh fruit. I'd say it's quite a healthy choice because it gives me enough energy for the whole morning."
* **Negative:** "Actually, I’m afraid I often just have a quick cup of coffee and some biscuits. I'm aware it's not the best choice, but I'm usually in a rush."

**Question 2: How often do you exercise? What kind of exercise do you enjoy the most?**

* **Positive:** "I try to exercise regularly, about three times a week. I personally find swimming very relaxing, and it helps me stay active without feeling too tired."
* **Neutral:** "Well, I don’t exercise as much as I should, but I do enjoy long walks in the evening. It might not be intense, but it's better than nothing, isn't it?"

**Question 3: How do you take care of your mental health?**

* **Positive:** "I believe taking time to relax is essential. I enjoy reading or meditating for twenty minutes; it really helps me manage stress."
* **Negative:** "To be fair, I sometimes find it hard to prioritize my mental health. I’m often quite busy, so I don't always have time to truly unwind."

### **5. Professional Scenarios (Dialogue Adaptation)**

**Scenario: Discussing Lifestyle Changes with a Colleague**

**Person A:** "Hi there! I couldn't help but notice you've been bringing healthy lunches lately. Are you trying a new routine?"

**Person B:** "Actually, yes! I've decided to focus more on a **balanced diet** and avoid **junk food** as much as possible. I’m also trying to **exercise regularly** before work."

**Person A:** "That sounds quite impressive. To be honest, I sometimes **skip meals** when the office gets busy, which I know is a bad habit."

**Person B:** "I understand, but you might find that eating small, healthy snacks helps you stay focused. Also, would you like to join me for a walk during the break? It’s a great way to **stay active**."

### **6. Polite English Cheat Sheet**

| Phrase | When to use it |
| --- | --- |
| **To be honest / To be fair** | Коли ви хочете висловити свою чесну думку, особливо якщо вона може бути негативною. |
| **Actually** | Використовується для ввічливого виправлення когось або додавання нової інформації. |
| **I’m afraid (that)...** | Ввічливий спосіб повідомити щось неприємне або визнати погану звичку. |
| **I’d say / I believe** | Робить вашу думку менш категоричною та більш професійною. |
| **Could you / Would you...** | Модальні дієслова для пом’якшення прохань або запитань. |
| **Might / It sounds like...** | Використовується для надання порад, щоб вони не звучали як прямий наказ. |


# What to Wear (Casual vs. Formal)

## 1. Vocabulary & Definitions

| Word/Phrase | Definition in English | Переклад |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **A T-shirt** | A casual short-sleeved cotton top. | Футболка |
| **Pants / Trousers** | An outer garment covering the body from the waist to the ankles. | Штани / Брюки |
| **A jumper / sweater** | A knitted garment worn over the upper body. | Джемпер / Светр |
| **A shirt** | A garment with a collar and buttons down the front. | Сорочка |
| **A suit** | A set of outer clothes made of the same fabric (jacket and trousers). | Костюм |
| **A dress** | A one-piece garment for women or girls that covers the body. | Сукня |
| **A jacket** | A short coat, often worn as part of a formal outfit. | Піджак / Куртка |
| **A tie** | A long piece of cloth worn around the collar and tied in a knot. | Краватка |
| **A tracksuit** | Loose clothes consisting of a top and trousers for exercise. | Спортивний костюм |
| **Formal shoes** | Elegant footwear worn for business or special events. | Мешти / Офіційне взуття |
| **Trainers / Sneakers** | Soft shoes suitable for sports or casual activities. | Кросівки |
| **A swimsuit** | Clothing worn for swimming. | Купальник |

## 2. Grammar Essentials: First Conditional

У цій лекції ми використовуємо **First Conditional** для планування вибору одягу залежно від ситуації чи погоди.

**Formula:** `If + Present Simple, I will + Verb`

* *Actually, if it rains tomorrow, I will take an umbrella.* (Насправді, якщо завтра піде дощ, я візьму парасольку).

**Key Points:**

* Ми використовуємо **will**, щоб виразити впевненість у майбутній дії.
* Щоб звучати ввічливіше, ми можемо додавати модальні дієслова: `If it's cold, I might wear a coat.` (Якщо буде холодно, я, можливо, одягну пальто).

## 3. Written Practice (Completed Exercises)

1. **Beach scenario:** If I go to the beach, I will wear a swimsuit and sunglasses.
2. **Business meeting:** If I have a business meeting, I will wear a suit and a tie.
3. **Winter weather:** If it is snowy and cold, I will wear a warm jumper and trousers.
4. **Nature walk:** If I go for a walk in the park, I will wear a tracksuit and trainers.
5. **Party time:** If I go to a party, I will wear a beautiful dress (or a smart jacket).
6. **At home:** If I stay at home to work, I will wear a comfortable T-shirt and pants.
7. **Rainy day:** If it rains, I will wear a waterproof jacket and take an umbrella.

## 4. Speaking & Discussion Guide

**Q: What will you wear for a dinner at a fancy restaurant?**

* **Positive:** To be honest, I would probably choose a classic dark suit or a formal dress, as I believe it’s important to respect the atmosphere of the place.
* **Neutral:** Well, it actually depends on the restaurant's dress code. I might wear a smart shirt and trousers, but nothing too restricted.
* **Negative:** I’m afraid I find formal clothes quite uncomfortable, so I would try to wear something casual-chic, like dark jeans and a nice jacket.

**Q: What will you wear when working from home?**

* **Positive:** I personally prefer wearing a comfortable jumper and soft pants. It really helps me stay focused and relaxed during the day.
* **Neutral:** Could I say it varies? If I have a video call, I will put on a professional shirt, but otherwise, I’ll stick to a T-shirt.
* **Negative:** To be fair, I’m not a fan of staying in pajamas all day. Even at home, I prefer wearing casual trousers to feel more organized.

## 5. Professional Dialogue Scenarios

**Scenario A: Preparing for a Business Trip**

* **Colleague A:** Could you tell me what the dress code for the conference is?
* **Colleague B:** Actually, it's quite formal. If you attend the gala dinner, you will need a suit.
* **Colleague A:** I see. I might bring my navy suit then. Would that be appropriate?
* **Colleague B:** Yes, that would be perfect. To be honest, most people will be dressed very professionally.

**Scenario B: Weekend Plans with a Friend**

* **Friend A:** If the weather is nice on Sunday, would you like to go for a hike?
* **Friend B:** I’d love to! I’ll probably wear my new trainers and a tracksuit.
* **Friend A:** Great idea. But if it gets a bit chilly, you might want to bring a jumper as well.
* **Friend B:** Thanks for the tip, I’ll keep that in mind.

## 6. Polite English Cheat Sheet

| Phrase / Softener | When to use it |
| --- | --- |
| **Actually / To be honest** | Використовуйте, щоб ввічливо висловити думку, яка може відрізнятися від очікуваної. |
| **I’m afraid...** | Використовуйте для пом'якшення негативної відповіді або відмови. |
| **Could you... / Would you...** | Використовуйте для створення ввічливих запитань замість наказового способу. |
| **I might / I would probably** | Додає відтінок невпевненості, що робить твердження менш категоричним. |
| **It depends on...** | Гарний спосіб почати розгорнуту відповідь, показуючи, що ви враховуєте різні обставини. |

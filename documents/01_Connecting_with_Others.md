#  Connecting with Others

## 1. Vocabulary & Definitions


| Word/Phrase | Definition in English | Переклад українською |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **How are you doing?** | A friendly way to ask how someone is feeling or how their life is going. | Як справи? |
| **Can't complain** | A response meaning things are going well or as expected. | Не можу скаржитися (все добре). |
| **Same old** | A way to say that nothing much has changed in your life. | Все по-старому. |
| **Come from** | To be born in a specific place or to have it as your home. | Бути родом з... |
| **Work as** | To have a particular job or profession. | Працювати (кимось). |
| **Journalist** | A person who writes news stories for newspapers, magazines, or TV. | Журналіст. |
| **A bit** | A small amount or degree. | Трохи. |

## 2. Grammar Essentials

На основі матеріалів лекції можна виділити такі граматичні конструкції для представлення себе та запитань.

* **Present Simple for General Facts:**
* *Formula:* Subject + Verb (s/es)
* 
*Example:* I **speak** two languages. 

* 
*Example:* Where **do** you **come from**? 

* **Present Continuous for Current Situations:**
* *Formula:* Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing
* 
*Example:* I **am living** in Canada now. 

* 
*Example:* Why **are** you **learning** English? 




* **Past Simple for Origins:**
* *Formula:* Subject + was/were + born
* 
*Example:* I **was born** in Kyiv.

## 3. Written Practice

* **Complete the phrases:**
1. How are you **doing**? 

2. Where do you **come from**? 

3. I was **born in** Spain. 

4. I **come from** Ukraine but now I’m **living in** Canada. 

5. What do **you do**? 

6. I **work as** a dentist. 

7. Why **are you learning** English? 

8. How **many languages do you** speak? 



* **Dialogue Completion:**
* **Elisa:** Hi! **How are you doing?** 

* **Peter:** I’m doing well, thanks. **How about** you? 
 
**Elisa:** **Not bad**, just a bit busy with work. 

* **Translation Block:**
 
*Чим ви займаєтесь?* — **What do you do?** 

*Скількома мовами ви розмовляєте?* — **How many languages do you speak?** 

*Я народився у Києві, але зараз живу у Львові.* — **I was born in Kyiv, but now I live in Lviv.**

## 4. Speaking & Discussion Guide

### Discussion Questions & Sample Answers


1. What questions do you ask when you meet a new person? 

* **Positive/Proactive:** "To be honest, I usually start by asking where they come from, as it's a great icebreaker."
* **Neutral:** "It depends on the situation, but I might ask what they do for a living if we are at a professional event."
* **Cautious:** "I’m afraid I’m a bit shy, so I typically wait for the other person to ask something first, like 'How are you doing?'."

2. Why are you learning English? 

* **Professional:** "Actually, I'm learning English because it's essential for my career growth in journalism."
* **Travel-oriented:** "I’d say I’m doing it mainly to feel more comfortable while traveling abroad."
* **Personal interest:** "To be fair, I just enjoy the process of learning and I'd like to watch movies in the original language."

3. How many languages do you speak? 

* **Confident:** "I’m proud to say I speak three languages: Ukrainian, English, and a bit of German."
* **Modest:** "I’m afraid I only speak two languages fluently, but I’m working hard on my English right now."

### Professional Scenarios

**Scenario: Networking at a Conference**

* **Participant A:** "Excuse me, I couldn't help but notice your presentation. How are you doing today?"
* **Participant B:** "I'm doing well, thank you for asking! It's been a bit of a busy morning, though."
* **Participant A:** "I can imagine. If you don't mind me asking, what do you do exactly?"
* **Participant B:** "Actually, I work as a software developer, but I'm originally from Ukraine."

## 5. Polite English Cheat Sheet

Список ввічливих фраз, використаних у конспекті.

| Phrase | When to use |
| --- | --- |
| **Actually / To be honest** | Використовуйте, щоб ввічливо додати уточнення або висловити думку, яка може відрізнятися. |
| **I’m afraid...** | Використовуйте для ввічливого висловлення жалю або коли ви змушені дати негативну відповідь. |
| **How about you?** | Чудовий спосіб повернути запитання співрозмовнику, щоб підтримати діалог. |
| **I’d say...** | Допомагає зробити ваше твердження менш категоричним та більш м'яким. |
| **If you don't mind me asking...** | Використовується перед тим, як поставити особисте запитання, щоб не здаватися грубим. |

### 2. Grammar Essentials (Граматика)

Матеріали фокусуються на використанні **Imperatives** (Наказового способу) для надання інструкцій та рецептів, а також на структурі **"Do not forget to..."** для нагадувань.

* **Надання інструкцій:** Використовуйте базову форму дієслова.
* *Example:* **Heat** some olive oil in the pan. **Add** some salt.


* **Нагадування:** "Do not forget + to-infinitive".
 
*Example:* **Do not forget to buy** rice noodles.

*Example:* **Do not forget to add** some salt.

**Exercise 1: Choose the correct word**

1. After cooking, you **drain** the water from the noodles.

### 4. Speaking & Discussion Guide

**Question 1: Do you like cooking?** 

* *(Positive):* **To be honest**, I absolutely love it. I find the process quite relaxing after a long day at work.
* *(Neutral):* **Actually**, I don't mind it, but I usually prefer simple recipes that don't take too much time.
* *(Negative):* **I’m afraid** I’m not very good at it. I tend to find the whole process a bit stressful, if I’m being truthful.

**Question 2: How often do you cook?** 

* **Sample Answer:** **Actually**, I try to cook at home at least four times a week because I believe homemade meals are much healthier. **However**, I might grab a takeaway if I'm particularly busy.

**Question 3: What is your favourite dish to cook?** 

* **Sample Answer:** **I would say** my favourite is a simple chicken stir-fry. It’s quite easy to prepare; you just need to slice the breast and mix it with some fresh veggies.

**Question 4: Do you prefer cooking at home or eating out?** 

* **Sample Answer:** **To be fair**, I enjoy both. **I would prefer** to eat out for special occasions, but for my daily routine, I’d rather cook at home as it’s more cost-effective.

**Question 5: Do you have a sweet tooth or do you prefer savoury meals?** 

* **Sample Answer:** **To be honest**, I definitely have a sweet tooth! I could never say no to a piece of chocolate cake. **However**, for dinner, I always go for something savoury.

### 5. Professional Scenarios (Сценарії діалогів)

**Scenario: Preparing a Healthy Dinner**

* **Anna:** Sasha, **could you please** pass me the knife? I need to slice this chicken breast.

* **Sasha:** **Certainly**, here you go. What’s the next step? 

**Anna:** We need to boil water in the pan for the rice noodles.

* **Sasha:** Okay, I'll handle that. How long should they cook? 

* **Anna:** Just about 5 minutes. After that, **would you mind** draining the water? 

* **Sasha:** Got it. I’ve already sliced the broccoli as well.

* **Anna:** Great! Let’s heat some olive oil in the pan, then we’ll add the noodles and chicken.

**Sasha:** It already smells wonderful! 

* **Anna:** Finally, let's mix everything and add a bit of soy sauce. **Actually**, it’s very simple.

### 6. Polite English Cheat Sheet

| Phrase | When to use |
| --- | --- |
| **To be honest / To be fair** | Використовуйте для вираження власної думки, особливо якщо вона може бути негативною. |
| **Actually** | Використовуйте для ввічливого уточнення або виправлення інформації. |
| **I’m afraid...** | Використовуйте перед тим, як сказати "ні" або повідомити щось неприємне. |
| **Could you...? / Would you mind...?** | Використовуйте для ввічливих прохань замість наказового способу. |
| **I would say... / I would prefer...** | Робить вашу думку менш категоричною та більш виваженою. |

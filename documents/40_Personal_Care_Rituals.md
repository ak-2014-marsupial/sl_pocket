### **2. Grammar Essentials: Daily Routines & Actions**

**To put on makeup / To wear makeup:** Ми використовуємо **"put on"** для процесу нанесення та **"wear"** для стану, коли макіяж вже на обличчі.

* *Example:* "I’m afraid I need a few more minutes to **put on** my makeup."

**To trim the beard:** Використовується для підтримки охайної форми бороди.

**To apply [product]:** Більш професійний синонім до "to put on" (використовується для кремів, сироваток, кондиціонерів).

* *Example:* "Could you please tell me how often I should **apply** this conditioner?"

### **3. Written Practice**

**Sarah’s routine:** Sarah starts her day with a refreshing shower where she uses a **body wash** to clean her skin. After her shower, she dries her hair and then straightens it with her **hair straightener**. Before going out, Sarah likes to put on makeup. She applies **foundation** and a touch of **blush** to her cheeks, giving her a fresh look.

* **John’s routine:** John takes his grooming seriously. He begins by using **shaving cream** and a **razor** to shave his face. After shaving, he trims his beard with a **beard trimmer** to keep it neat. John finishes his routine by applying a light spritz of his favorite **cologne**.

* **Mike’s routine:** Mike prefers a quick and simple routine. He uses **shampoo** and **hair conditioner** to keep his hair clean and smooth. After his shower, he dries his hair with a **hair dryer**. Also, he uses **cologne** to smell great.

**Lisa’s routine:** On weekends, Lisa begins with a long shower using **body wash** to feel refreshed. She enjoys wearing makeup and spends time applying **foundation**, **mascara**, and **lipstick**. Lisa finishes by styling her hair with a **hair straightener** and adding a spritz of **perfume**.

### **4. Speaking & Discussion Guide**

**Q1: What is your daily personal care routine?** 

* **Positive/Detailed:** "To be honest, I try to keep it quite simple. I usually start with a quick shower using a mild body wash, and then I apply some moisturizer. Actually, I find that a consistent routine helps me feel more prepared for the day."
* **Neutral:** "Well, I wouldn’t say I have a very complex ritual. I just use the basics like shampoo and a bit of cologne. It doesn't take much time, which is quite convenient for my schedule."

Q2: Which personal care products are essential for you? Why? 

* **Positive:** "Actually, a good hair conditioner is absolutely essential for me because it makes my hair much easier to manage. I also believe that a quality perfume is important for a professional impression."
* **Negative/Minimalist:** "To be fair, I’m not really into many products. Perhaps just a reliable razor and a simple face wash are all I truly need to feel comfortable."

**Q3: How often do you use beauty gadgets, and how do they help you?** 

* **Detailed:** "I might use a hair dryer almost every day, especially when I’m in a rush. Occasionally, I also use a beard trimmer to keep things looking neat. It’s actually much more efficient than going to a barber every week."

### **5. Practice Dialogues (Scenarios)**

**Scenario A: Choosing a Gift (Based on the Birthday Wishlist)** 

* **Person A:** "I’m actually planning to get a birthday gift for my friend. I notice she has a **cosmetic bag** and some **mascara** on her wishlist. Would you happen to know which brand she prefers?"
* **Person B:** "To be honest, I’m not entirely sure, but she did mention she likes red **lipstick**. Perhaps we could look for a nice set together?"

**Scenario B: Morning Prep in a Shared Space**

* **Person A:** "I’m afraid I’ll be a while in the bathroom. I still need to use the **hair dryer** and **straightener**."
* **Person B:** "That’s perfectly fine. Could you possibly let me know when you're finished? I just need to quickly use my **beard trimmer**."

### **6. Polite English Cheat Sheet**

| Phrase / Softener | When to use |
| --- | --- |
| **"Actually..."** | Щоб ввічливо уточнити інформацію або висловити іншу думку. |
| **"To be honest / To be fair..."** | Коли ви ділитеся особистою думкою, яка може бути суб'єктивною. |
| **"I’m afraid..."** | Для пом'якшення поганих новин або відмови (наприклад, "Я боюсь, це займе час"). |
| **"Perhaps / Might..."** | Щоб зробити ваші пропозиції або твердження менш категоричними. |
| **"Could you possibly...?"** | Дуже ввічлива форма запиту (Indirect question). |
| **"I wouldn’t say..."** | Спосіб висловити незгоду або заперечення без грубості. |

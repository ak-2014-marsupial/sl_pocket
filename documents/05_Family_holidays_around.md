# Family holidays around the world #
### 1. Vocabulary & Definitions

| Word/Phrase | Definition in English | Переклад українською |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Family traditions** | Activities that families do together every holiday.  | Сімейні традиції |
| **To celebrate** | To enjoy a special day or event with specific activities. | Святкувати |
| **To exchange gifts** | To give and get presents from other people.  | Обмінюватися подарунками |
| **To get together** | To meet and spend time with your family or friends. | Збиратися разом |
| **Traditional dishes** | Special food prepared specifically for holidays. | Традиційні страви |
| **To decorate** | To put ornaments and lights on something (e.g., a tree) to make it look pretty. | Прикрашати |
| **Baubles** | Round ornaments used to decorate a Christmas tree.  | Ялинкові кульки |
| **String lights** | Small lights on a wire used for holiday decoration. | Гірлянда |
| **Easter basket** | A basket filled with treats, eggs, and sweets for Easter. | Великодній кошик |
| **Fireworks** | Bright, colorful lights that explode in the sky during celebrations. | Феєрверки |
| **To bring joy** | To make people feel very happy.  | Приносити радість |

---

### 2. Grammar Essentials: Prepositions of Time

* **AT** — використовуємо для назви самого свята як періоду:
* *Example:* **At** Christmas, **at** Easter.

* **ON** — використовуємо, коли говоримо про конкретний день свята:
* *Example:* **On** Christmas Day, **on** Easter Day.

### 4. Speaking & Discussion Guide

**Q: What is your favorite holiday and why?** 

* **Positive:** "Actually, I'm quite fond of Christmas. It’s a wonderful time to get together with my loved ones, and to be honest, I really enjoy the cozy atmosphere."
* **Neutral:** "I would say that New Year is probably my favorite. It’s a nice opportunity to reflect on the year, although it can be a bit tiring sometimes."

**Q: What family traditions do you have?** 

* **Option 1:** "In my family, we have a tradition of decorating the tree together. It might sound simple, but it truly brings us a lot of joy."
* **Option 2:** "I’m afraid we don’t have many strict traditions, but we usually try to have a large dinner on Christmas Eve."

**Q: What traditional dishes do you eat during holidays?** 

* **Answer:** "During Christmas, we usually prepare twelve traditional dishes. One of the most important is kutia. To be honest, it’s quite a long process to cook everything, but it's definitely worth it."

**Q: Do you exchange gifts with your friends or family?** 

* **Answer:** "Yes, we certainly do. We usually exchange gifts on Christmas morning. Could I just add that it’s more about the thought than the gift itself?"

### 6. Polite English Cheat Sheet

| Phrase/Softeners | When to use |
| --- | --- |
| **"Actually..."** | Коли хочете ввічливо додати інформацію або трохи виправити співрозмовника. |
| **"To be honest..."** | Використовуйте, щоб висловити свою справжню думку в м'якій формі. |
| **"I’m afraid..."** | Коли потрібно ввічливо повідомити про щось негативне або відмовити. |
| **"Could you perhaps..."** | Ввічливе прохання (замість прямого наказу). |
| **"I would say that..."** | Робить вашу думку менш категоричною та більш професійною. |
| **"It might sound..."** | Використовується, щоб пом'якшити наступне твердження. |

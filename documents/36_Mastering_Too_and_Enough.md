
# Mastering "Too" and "Enough"

## 1. Vocabulary & Definitions


| Word / Phrase | Definition in English | Переклад українською |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Too** | More than what is needed or wanted; to an excessive degree. | Занадто, надмірно |
| **Enough** | As much as necessary; in the required amount or degree. | Достатньо, досить |
| **Crowded** | Full of people, leaving little or no room for movement. | Багатолюдний, переповнений |
| **To react** | To respond to a particular situation or event. | Реагувати |
| **To concentrate** | To focus all one's attention on a particular object or activity. | Зосереджуватися |
| **Yummy** | Highly attractive or pleasing in taste; delicious. | Смачний, апетитний |
| **Rude** | Offensively impolite or ill-mannered. | Грубий, неввічливий |

---

## 2. Grammar Essentials

### **A. Using "TOO" (Negative Connotation)**

Використовується, коли чогось більше, ніж потрібно.

* **Formula 1:** `Too + Adjective/Adverb`

*Example:* The coffee is **too hot** to drink.

* **Formula 2:** `Too many + Countable Nouns` (з обчислювальними іменниками)
 
*Example:* There are **too many people**.

* **Formula 3:** `Too much + Uncountable Nouns` (з необчислювальними іменниками)
 
*Example:* There is **too much sauce**.


 
**Formula 4:** `Too few / Too little` (замало).

### **B. Using "ENOUGH" (Positive/Neutral Connotation)**

* **Formula 1:** `Adjective/Adverb + Enough`
 
*Example:* He is **tall enough** to reach the shelf.


* **Formula 2:** `Enough + Noun`
 
*Example:* We have **enough chairs**.

## 3. Written Practice (Completed Exercises)

### **Exercise 1: Remake using "TOO"**

1. The room is too cold for me to stay here.

2. The book is too difficult to understand it.

3. The noise is too loud to concentrate.

4. The car is too expensive to buy it.


### **Exercise 2: Quantifiers (Too many/much/few/little)**

1. There are **too many** people at the party. It’s too crowded!.

2. He added **too much** pepper to the dish, and now it’s spicy.

3. We had **too few** chairs for everyone, so some people had to stand.

4. I spent **too little** time preparing, so I failed the exam.


### **Exercise 3: Mixed Practice (Too / Enough)**

* This bag is **too** heavy for me to carry alone.

* She didn’t study **enough** to pass the test.

* It’s **too** cold to go outside without a coat.

* He is tall **enough** to reach the top shelf.

* The soup isn’t hot **enough** to eat yet.



### **Translation Block **

1. Це сталося занадто швидко, тому я не встиг зреагувати — *It happened too quickly, so I didn't have time to react*.

2. Я заробляю достатньо грошей — *I earn enough money*.

## 4. Speaking & Discussion Guide

### **Discussion Questions & Sample Answers**

**Q1: Why didn’t you buy that car?** 

* *(Negative/Economic):* **To be honest**, it was **too expensive** for my current budget, and I'd rather not overspend.
* *(Neutral):* **Actually**, it wasn't **practical enough** for my family's needs, although I liked the color.

**Q2: Why can't she play basketball?** 

* *(Direct):* **I’m afraid** she isn’t **tall enough** to play at a professional level, but she is a great coach.
* *(Softened):* **Actually**, she’s **too busy** with her studies at the moment to join the team.

**Q3: Why did she fail the test?** 

* *(Constructive):* **It seems** she spent **too little** time preparing, so she wasn't quite ready for the difficult questions.
* *(Neutral):* **To be honest**, the exam was **too difficult** for everyone in the class.

## 5. Professional Dialogues (Adapted Scenarios)

**Scenario 1: At a Restaurant (Topic: Too much / Enough)**

* **Customer:** "Excuse me, I'm afraid this soup is **too spicy** for me to eat. Could I possibly have a glass of water?"
* **Waiter:** "I'm so sorry to hear that. I'll bring some water immediately. Would you like me to replace the dish with something less hot?"
* **Customer:** "That's very kind of you, but I think the water will be **enough**, thank you."

**Scenario 2: Job Interview (Topic: Enough skills)**

* **Interviewer:** "Do you think you have **enough experience** for this managerial position?"
* **Candidate:** "**Actually**, I believe my five years in the industry are **sufficient enough** to handle these responsibilities, although I'm always eager to learn more."

## 6. Polite English Cheat Sheet

| Softener / Structure | When to use | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Actually / To be honest** | Щоб ввічливо не погодитися або повідомити неприємну новину. | *Actually, it’s too loud here.* |
| **I’m afraid...** | Щоб вказати на проблему або відмовити. | *I'm afraid we don't have enough time.* |
| **Could / Would / Might** | Модальні дієслова для пом'якшення прохань. | *Would you mind? It's too cold.* |
| **It seems / I believe...** | Щоб ваша думка не звучала надто категорично. | *It seems the car is too expensive.* |
| **Not quite... enough** | Ввічливий спосіб сказати "замало". | *The room is not quite big enough.* |
